How the Risk Engine Works

This platform predicts the Probability of Default (PD), which represents the likelihood that a borrower will fail to repay a loan. The lower the PD, the safer the applicant.

The engine uses XGBoost, a powerful gradient boosting model widely used in banking, fintech, and credit scoring systems due to its ability to model complex non-linear risk patterns.

Instead of simple rule-based credit checks, the model evaluates dozens of financial, behavioural and credit-history signals to produce a statistically optimized risk score.

Risk Bands & Business Decisions

  • Low Risk — Borrowers with low PD. These customers are safe and can be automatically approved.
  • Medium Risk — Moderate PD. These cases are routed for manual review by credit officers.
  • High Risk — High PD. These borrowers have a high chance of default and are automatically rejected.

This risk-based automation mirrors how modern banks and NBFCs make lending decisions.
