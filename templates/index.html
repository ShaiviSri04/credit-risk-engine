Credit Risk Decision Engine

AI-powered loan risk scoring platform

What this system does

This platform predicts the Probability of Default (PD) for loan applicants using XGBoost, a high-performance machine learning model trained on real financial data.

Risk Interpretation

Applicants are grouped into Low, Medium or High risk based on PD.

  • Low Risk → Approve
  • Medium Risk → Manual Review
  • High Risk → Reject

Why this model is strong

  • XGBoost trained on real banking attributes
  • Evaluated using ROC-AUC, PR-AUC, KS metrics
  • Designed for imbalanced credit risk data
  • Business-aligned risk banding
